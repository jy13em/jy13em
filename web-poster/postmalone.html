POST MALONE

GRAMMY nominated, multi-platinum artist Post Malone revealed the Runaway Tour, his latest North American outing kicking off later this year. Produced by Live Nation, the run will start September 14 in Tacoma, WA and visit cities across the U.S. and Canada before wrapping November 20 at The Forum in Los Angeles.

Austin Richard Post, better known as Post Malone, launched into stardom after the release of his first single, "White Iverson," in 2015. The Texas-native has since released chart-topping tracks, including the smash-hits "Rockstar" and "Psycho." Two albums later, Post Malone has taken over the radiowaves and staked his claim in the hip-hop world as a Grammy-nominated artist. In addition to guest starring on songs with Kanye West and Young Thug, Post Malone has become a prolific hip-hop producer and songwriter. After touring with Justin Bieber, the rapper is ready to hit the road on his 2019 Runaway tour. When you buy Post Malone tickets, you can expect to experience a night filled with surprises and unforgettable music.
